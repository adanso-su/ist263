Welcome to Devas, A dragon like fictional species, created by Lovelessism!

Hated by the gods, they seek vengeance.
The children that god left behind.


Once a deity, now just a worthless insect.
They were told that their mother was a traitor to the gods, so the gods made her jump into the abyss.
Her body shattered into pieces and scattered around the earth.
From all of her fragments, Devas were born.
